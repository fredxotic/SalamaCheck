About SalamaCheck

SalamaCheck was built on the core principle that digital safety should be accessible and unintrusive. Our technology delivers accurate risk assessments in real-time without storing user data, offering a secure and transparent service.

Under the Hood
  • AI-powered contextual threat detection.
  • Strict privacy-first policy: no data storage or logging.
